Vintage Cars For The Ultimate Road Trip

1

Jaguar E-Type

Jaguar E-Type once held the enviable title of the most beautiful car in the world and one of the lucky few to be inducted into the New York Museum of Art. Its aesthetics and unique styling are sure to turn heads and draw admiration from onlookers during your next road trip. Beyond the famed beauty, this gem is quite a performer thanks to the 3.8-liter engine that can reach a top speed of 150mph.

2

Mercedes-Benz 190SL

Mercedes-Benz 190SL boast a long history as one of the ideal choice for road trips thanks to its powerful engine, excellent handle, and fun aesthetics. It was introduced in 1955 as the more affordable model of its predecessor, although it quickly turned into a symbol of luxury. The 190SL is convertible to let you take the roof down and enjoy amazing views amid a cool breeze on a hot summer road trip. It boasts a curved chassis body and a comfortable and spacious leather interior to accommodate all your extra bags.

3

Chevrolet Corvette Z06

Chevy Z06 can be traced back nearly six decades ago when legendary Corvette engineer Zora Arkus-Duntov developed it as an option package for enthusiasts who wanted to race their rides on the track. It combines striking aesthetics with high-performance engineering, making it a beautiful and iconic sports car worth taking on long road trips. The Z06 boasts incredible performance capabilities powered by a solid engine and unmatched aerodynamic design that delivers elegance with a touch of aggression.

4

1970 Alfa Romeo Spider

This lightweight two-seater remains one of the most beloved classic sports cars despite going through diverse interactions across the series. The Alfa Romeo Spider comes with a classic and iconic convertible body style with a soft-top roof and timeless design, making it a pure joy to drive. It is powered by a 1.8-liter inline-four-cylinder engine producing around 118 horsepower (88 kW) and mated to a 5-speed manual transmission. The Spider is ideal for those long summer drives through rolling countryside scenes.

5

Chevrolet Suburban Z71

There is simply nothing more American than going on a road trip in a Suburban. This massive SUV boasts a long history spanning over 80 years. Although it is a bit slow, the Suburban has several tricks up its sleeve. It is designed to be a people carrier and an ultimate road trip ride with ample legroom and storage space. What is more, the Z71 has an AWD drivetrain that provides better traction on various road conditions.
